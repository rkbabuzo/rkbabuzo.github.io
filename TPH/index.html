DESIGN BRIEF

For this exercise, I explored more on the creative direction rather than the technical aspect of the design. I used storytelling as a foundation to improve the chances of readers remaining on the page. I believe storytelling is more effective than plain format.

I also utilized design trends that are influenced by technology, media, and the fashion industry. This includes the use of bold colors, animations, custom illustrations, parallax scrolling, and semi-flat designs.

 

What's great about Thumbtack's visuals especially those found on blogs, is that it prioritizes on content rather than focusing too much on consistent branding. Thumbtack adapts designs based on topics and trends as shown on the images on the left.

Concept 1: Landing Page Type

Newsletter Web Page (Use email as landing page):

For content heavy newsletters, I think it would be best to open the rest of the content on a separate web page. When sending email through MailChimp, long content gets truncated and HTML and CSS features are very limited.

 

Although it is still possible to send long formats by different methods, the time it takes in preparing the HTML email is similar to creating a full web page. By utilizing web pages, we can take advantage of its array of technical features in delivering interactive and engaging content.

 

We can also deploy Google Analytics to help improve our content and better understand our  readers.

NEWS LETTER DESIGN NOTES:

Videos & Animations (TPH Text Support):

For the past year, there's an increase in demand for quality videos that tell stories. Aside from photos, producing creatively directed and edited short videos and animations can keep readers interested.

 

Improving visual impact of photographs (Member Spotlight):

By making small changes in the photos, we can significantly improve the visual content. We can achieve this through supplying simple instructions to members who will be featured on the newsletter. The instruction can include a photo reference, camera angle, posing, and type of lighting. We can also embed Instagram posts.

 

Making content more engaging (Blogs):

Apart from creating interesting titles, the copy should tell a story. Making the content more purposeful, personal, and relevant can improve reader engagement.
